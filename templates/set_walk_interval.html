ℹ️ About Walk Interval

The walk interval determines how frequently torrt checks for updates to your registered torrents.

Recommendations:
  • Frequent updates (1-3 hours): For time-sensitive content like daily TV shows
  • Regular updates (6-12 hours): Good balance for most content
  • Infrequent updates (24+ hours): For content that updates slowly or to save resources
How it works:
  1. torrt checks all registered torrents at the specified interval
  2. If updates are found, they're automatically added to your torrent client
  3. You can also manually trigger updates from the main page
Note: Setting very short intervals (less than 1 hour) may put excessive load on tracker sites.
